Front & back of house

POS built for real service — not just order entry

From the first tap to the last payment, every step is tracked for analytics and kitchen execution.

  • Serve flow — hold tickets, fire courses, mark ready & served, then pay
  • Split checks — by seat or item, cash/card/mobile on the same check
  • Fractional modifiers — half-and-half pizza toppings and combo splits
  • Station routing — grill, fry, cold, pizza, service bar, expo
  • KDS display — kitchen sees only what matters at each station
  • Recipe depletion — firing an order deducts exact ingredient quantities from inventory
